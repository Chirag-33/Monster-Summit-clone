Why Attend Monster Scale Summit

The Monster Scale Summit community is all about discussing strategies for
meeting performance expectations at extreme scale. Whether you’re
designing, implementing, or optimizing systems that are pushed to their
limits, we’d love to hear about your most impressive achievements and
lessons learned.

Core topics include:

Distributed databases
Streaming and real-time processing
intriguing systems designs
SRE techniques proven at scale
Methods for balancing latency/concurrency/throughput
Infrastructure built for unprecedented demands
